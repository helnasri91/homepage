From the big apple to Canada and finally situated in Los Angeles city.
The son of two lovely Sudanese natives whom immigrated to New-York City
in 1988. Shortly afterwards my family established itself in Montreal and then
later Vancouver In my youth, I was the IT person(pun intended)
and spent a lot of time making websites of comics and movies
like Dragonball. My father and I worked around a lot of network
upgrades with hardware from cisco networking systems although most of my assistance
was menial. We aided the network upgrades of mostly muslims schools
and interestingly farms within the province of British-Columbia I predominantly
worked in restaurants and IT after high-school keeping myself curious
and hungry I travelled all over North America and Recently made a trip to South Korea.
Currently I'm community college student in Santa Monica with my sights
on the Electrical Engineering and Computer Science program over at Berkeley or Cal State Pomona.
Fun facts about me outside of technical interest; I play a mean jazzy blues guitar,
a decorated amateur boxer, and I speak about 4 languages(human languages). Future aspirations
are to grow professionally in the United States as an engineer and to then take what I've learned
and apply it globally to places in need, such as my folks' place
in the Sudan. One of my mantras in terms of growth knowledge
and opportunity is to remember, I am not okay until we are all okay!
